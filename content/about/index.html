Introduction

EasyRPG is a project that aims to create a free (as in freedom) cross-platform and multi-language Role Playing Game (RPG) creation tool, inspired by a commercial product called RPG Maker.

The project is composed of several parts:

  • EasyRPG Player
  • RTP Replacement
  • Tools
  • EasyRPG Editor

Main goals

These are our main goals:

  • Free as in freedom: Every component of the project will be published under a free license.
  • Cross-platform: We want EasyRPG to reach as many platforms as possible.
  • Multi-language: The more languages are supported, the better.

Contribute to the project

This is a free and opensource (FLOSS) project, so we would be glad to receive contributions. Anyone can help, from programmers or artists to testers and translators.
